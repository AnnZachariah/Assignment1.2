Founded by the Society of Franciscan Brothers, St. Francis Institute of Technology was established in the year 1999. This institution is affiliated with the University of Mumbai and has received approvals from AICTE. Presently, this institution offers nine different programmes at the undergraduate, doctoral and postgraduate level. Since its inception, it has been among the top six colleges under the University of Mumbai. This institution has approximately 1811 students studying in it. Additionally, SIFT has six academic departments, including the Department of Information Technology, Department of Computer Engineering and Department of Electrical Engineering. This institution has also received approvals from reputable organisations, including NBA and ISO.
This college has an E-Cell, which focuses on promoting and nurturing young entrepreneurs. The objectives of this cell are to mentor new and budding start-ups, nurturing and fostering ideas and imparting entrepreneurial skills. This cell organises several events such as Pragati and Unnati, where the college students compete with each other with their technological innovations. In 2017, this cell established a National Entrepreneurship Network, which supports student entrepreneurs, SMEs and start-ups for creating employment in Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Kenya, India and Mexico. This network runs courses based on entrepreneurship along with the regular college courses.
